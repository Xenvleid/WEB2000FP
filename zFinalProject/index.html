Welcome to the music library

These are some of my favorite albums of all time, mostly because of my parents. My dad always played Camilo Sesto during my childhood. That being said, I can honestly say that I can most likely sing along with about 80% of his songs. Julio Iglesias was my mother favorite, although I can't say I know almost any of his songs. However, these are the songs I grew up with and instilled in me a sense of appreciation for classic Spanish ballads.
